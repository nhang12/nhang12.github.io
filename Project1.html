Project 2

Mini Sidescroller Metroidvania

  • A simple sidescrolling minigame where you control a knight and slay various enemies across the stage.

  • Game maker is a versatile development tool that helps beginners and experts in creating video games of their own. This will be where the game is mostly developed. That and with some spritework they offer, I hope to create at least a single level where you fight monsters in a dungeon.

We're already taking our first few steps!

Our next steps...

  • After that comes a ground surface in which the PC (Playable Character) can traverse on. This is to limit the playstyle and allow for enemies and pickups to stay for the player to interact with.

  • Then, we would tinker with more fundamental movesets, such as attack, jump, crouch, slide, and taunt.

  • Finally, we would then input basic NPCs for the player to attack and interact with, interacting props. After the main work is finished, then the polishing and animation!
